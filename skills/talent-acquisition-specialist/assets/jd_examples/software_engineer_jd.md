# Job Description: Software Engineer

## Position Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Job Title | Software Engineer |
| Department | Engineering |

| Location | San Francisco, CA (Hybrid - 3 days in office) |
| Employment Type | Full-time |

---

## Position Summary

We are seeking a talented Software Engineer to join our Product Engineering team. In this role, you will design, develop, and maintain scalable software solutions that power our core platform. You will work closely with product managers, designers, and fellow engineers to deliver features that delight our users.

### Why This Role Matters

Our platform serves millions of users daily, and every line of code you write directly impacts their experience. You will have the opportunity to solve complex technical challenges, contribute to architectural decisions, and shape the future of our technology stack.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Primary Duties (70%)

1. **Design & Development**
   - Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
   - Design and implement new features and services
   - Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback

2. **Technical Problem Solving**
   - Debug and resolve complex production issues
   - Optimize system performance and scalability
   - Evaluate and integrate third-party services and tools

3. **Collaboration**
   - Work with product and design teams to refine requirements
   - Collaborate on technical specifications and architecture documents
   - Mentor junior engineers through pair programming and code reviews

### Secondary Duties (30%)

1. **On-Call Support**
   - Participate in on-call rotation (approximately 1 week per month)
   - Respond to production incidents and perform root cause analysis

2. **Continuous Improvement**
   - Identify and address technical debt
   - Contribute to engineering best practices and documentation

---

## Requirements

### Required Qualifications

**Experience:**
- 3+ years of professional software development experience
- Experience with at least one backend language (Python, Java, Go, or similar)
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL)
- Experience with version control systems (Git)

**Skills:**
- Strong understanding of data structures and algorithms
- Familiarity with RESTful API design principles
- Experience with automated testing (unit, integration)
- Comfortable working in Unix/Linux environments

### Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines
- Experience with front-end technologies (React, TypeScript)
- Contributions to open-source projects
