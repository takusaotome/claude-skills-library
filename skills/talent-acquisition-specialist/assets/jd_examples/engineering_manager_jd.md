## Position Summary

We are seeking an experienced Engineering Manager to lead our Platform Engineering team. In this role, you will be responsible for building and developing a high-performing engineering team, driving technical excellence, and delivering critical platform capabilities that power our entire product suite.

### Why This Role Matters

As an Engineering Manager, you will shape both the technical direction and the culture of our engineering organization. You will balance people leadership with technical strategy, ensuring your team delivers high-quality solutions while growing their careers. The systems your team builds will be the foundation for all of our products.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### People Leadership (50%)

1. **Team Development**
   - Hire, develop, and retain a team of 6-10 engineers
   - Conduct regular 1:1s, career conversations, and performance reviews
   - Create growth opportunities and development plans for each team member
   - Foster an inclusive environment where everyone can do their best work

2. **Team Performance**
   - Set clear expectations and objectives for the team
   - Remove blockers and provide resources for team success
   - Address performance issues promptly and constructively
   - Celebrate wins and recognize contributions

### Technical Leadership (30%)

1. **Technical Strategy**
   - Partner with architects on system design and technical decisions
   - Balance technical debt reduction with feature development
   - Establish and maintain engineering standards and best practices
   - Evaluate and recommend technology investments

2. **Delivery Excellence**
   - Ensure on-time delivery of high-quality software
   - Drive operational excellence (reliability, performance, security)
   - Participate in on-call escalations and incident reviews
   - Monitor and improve team productivity metrics

### Cross-Functional Collaboration (20%)

1. **Stakeholder Management**
   - Partner with Product on roadmap planning and prioritization
   - Communicate progress, risks, and dependencies to leadership
   - Coordinate with other engineering teams on cross-team initiatives

2. **Process Improvement**
   - Continuously improve development processes and workflows
   - Lead retrospectives and implement improvements
   - Contribute to engineering-wide initiatives and programs

**Skills:**
- Strong technical background with ability to engage in architectural discussions
- Excellent communication skills (written, verbal, presentations)

- Data-driven approach to decision making

### Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with distributed systems and cloud infrastructure
- Experience scaling engineering teams during growth periods
- Background in platform/infrastructure engineering
- Experience in a high-growth startup environment

## Success Metrics

### First 30 Days
- Complete manager onboarding program
- Conduct 1:1s with all team members
- Understand current projects, challenges, and opportunities
- Build relationships with cross-functional partners

### First 60 Days
- Establish team rhythms (1:1s, team meetings, planning)
- Identify quick wins and improvement opportunities
- Participate in hiring activities
- Deliver first objective assessment of team health

### First 90 Days
- Complete first planning cycle with the team
- Address any performance or team dynamic issues
- Propose and begin implementing process improvements
- Establish trusted advisor relationships with stakeholders
