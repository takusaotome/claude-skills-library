---

## Position Summary

We are looking for a driven Sales Representative to join our Enterprise Sales team. In this role, you will be responsible for generating new business and managing relationships with mid-market and enterprise accounts. You will own the full sales cycle from prospecting to close and work closely with our Customer Success team to ensure long-term client success.

### Why This Role Matters

Our customers rely on our platform to transform their businesses. As a Sales Representative, you are the first point of contact for potential customers and play a critical role in helping them understand how our solutions can solve their challenges. Your success directly drives company growth and customer impact.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Primary Duties (80%)

1. **New Business Development**
   - Identify and qualify new business opportunities through outbound prospecting
   - Conduct discovery calls to understand customer needs and challenges
   - Deliver compelling product demonstrations and presentations
   - Develop and execute account strategies to win new business

2. **Pipeline Management**
   - Maintain accurate and up-to-date records in Salesforce CRM
   - Manage a pipeline of 3-4x quota coverage
   - Provide accurate weekly and monthly forecasts
   - Progress deals through defined sales stages

3. **Relationship Building**
   - Build relationships with key decision-makers and influencers
   - Navigate complex organizational structures
   - Partner with Solution Engineers for technical discovery
   - Collaborate with Customer Success for smooth handoffs

### Secondary Duties (20%)

1. **Market Intelligence**
   - Track competitive landscape and market trends
   - Share customer insights with Product and Marketing teams

2. **Team Contribution**
   - Participate in team meetings and knowledge sharing
   - Contribute to sales playbook and best practices

## Requirements

### Required Qualifications

**Experience:**
- 3+ years of B2B sales experience, preferably SaaS
- Track record of meeting or exceeding quota (President's Club or equivalent)
- Experience selling to mid-market or enterprise accounts ($50K+ deal sizes)
- Experience with CRM systems (Salesforce preferred)

**Skills:**
- Strong verbal and written communication skills
- Excellent presentation and demo skills
- Ability to manage multiple opportunities simultaneously
- Consultative selling approach

### Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in [specific industry: financial services, healthcare, etc.]
- Familiarity with solution selling methodologies (MEDDIC, Challenger, etc.)
- Experience with sales engagement tools (Outreach, SalesLoft, etc.)
- Existing network in target industries
