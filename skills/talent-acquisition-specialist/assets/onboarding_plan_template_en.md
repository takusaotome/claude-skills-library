## 4. 30-60-90 Day Plan

### Phase 1: Learning (Days 1-30)

**Theme**: Understand the organization and build foundation

| Week | Focus Area | Key Activities | Success Criteria |
|------|------------|----------------|------------------|
| 1 | Orientation | Team introductions, tool setup, basic training | Onboarding checklist complete |
| 2 | Learning | Meeting attendance, documentation review | Required training complete |
| 3 | Exploration | Understand workflows, identify stakeholders | Key process map created |
| 4 | Integration | Participate in meetings, contribute to discussions | Active participation observed |

**30-Day Milestones:**
- [ ] Can articulate company mission and values
- [ ] Regular communication with key team members
- [ ] Understands basic processes for role
- [ ] All mandatory training completed

---

### Phase 2: Contributing (Days 31-60)

**Theme**: Begin creating value through practice

| Week | Focus Area | Key Activities | Success Criteria |
|------|------------|----------------|------------------|
| 5-6 | Small Wins | Complete independent tasks, deliver outputs | First deliverable completed |
| 7-8 | Expanding | Take on larger tasks, incorporate feedback | Positive team feedback |

**60-Day Milestones:**
- [ ] Multiple work tasks completed independently
- [ ] Contributing to team's regular operations
- [ ] Proactively asking questions and suggesting improvements
- [ ] Receiving positive feedback from manager and peers

---

### Phase 3: Performing (Days 61-90)

**Theme**: Contribute at full speed and deliver results

| Week | Focus Area | Key Activities | Success Criteria |
|------|------------|----------------|------------------|
| 9-10 | Ownership | Own projects or processes | Independent work management |
| 11-12 | Impact | Achieve measurable results | Initial performance goals met |

**90-Day Milestones:**
- [ ] Performing role independently
- [ ] Meeting expected outcomes
- [ ] Recognized as part of the team
- [ ] Clear career goals established
