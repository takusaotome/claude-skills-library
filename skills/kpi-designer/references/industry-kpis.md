# Industry and Department KPI Reference

Common KPIs organized by industry and functional department.

## SaaS / Software Industry

### Company-Level KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) | Sum of all recurring revenue | Lagging | Growth 20-40% YoY |
| Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) | ARR / 12 | Lagging | Growth 3-5% MoM |
| Churn Rate | Customers Lost / Total Customers | Lagging | <5% annual |
| Net Revenue Retention (NRR) | (Starting MRR + Expansion - Churn) / Starting MRR | Lagging | >110% |
| Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | Sales & Marketing Spend / New Customers | Leading | <12 month payback |
| Lifetime Value (LTV) | ARPU × Avg Lifespan | Lagging | LTV:CAC > 3:1 |
| Net Promoter Score (NPS) | % Promoters - % Detractors | Leading | >40 |

### Product KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Daily Active Users (DAU) | Unique users per day | Leading | Depends on product |
| Monthly Active Users (MAU) | Unique users per month | Leading | Growth 5-10% MoM |
| DAU/MAU Ratio (Stickiness) | DAU / MAU | Leading | >20% |
| Feature Adoption Rate | Users Using Feature / Total Users | Leading | >60% within 90 days |
| Time to Value | Days from signup to first value | Leading | <7 days |

## E-commerce / Retail

### Company-Level KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) | Total sales value | Lagging | Growth 15-25% YoY |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Total Revenue / Number of Orders | Lagging | Depends on category |
| Conversion Rate | Orders / Visitors | Leading | 2-5% |
| Cart Abandonment Rate | Abandoned Carts / Total Carts | Leading | <70% |
| Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) | AOV × Purchase Frequency × Lifespan | Lagging | CLV:CAC > 3:1 |
| Repeat Purchase Rate | Repeat Customers / Total Customers | Lagging | >30% |

### Operations KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Inventory Turnover | COGS / Average Inventory | Leading | 4-6x per year |
| Fill Rate | Orders Filled / Orders Received | Leading | >95% |
| On-Time Delivery | On-Time Deliveries / Total Deliveries | Leading | >98% |
| Return Rate | Returns / Total Orders | Lagging | <5% |

## Manufacturing

### Production KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) | Availability × Performance × Quality | Leading | >85% |
| First Pass Yield (FPY) | Good Units / Total Units Produced | Leading | >95% |
| Cycle Time | Total Time / Units Produced | Leading | Minimize |
| Scrap Rate | Scrap Units / Total Units | Lagging | <2% |
| On-Time In-Full (OTIF) | Perfect Orders / Total Orders | Lagging | >95% |

### Quality KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Defects Per Million (DPMO) | (Defects / Opportunities) × 1,000,000 | Lagging | <3.4 (Six Sigma) |
| Cost of Poor Quality (COPQ) | Internal + External Failure Costs | Lagging | <1% of revenue |
| Customer Complaints | Number of complaints per period | Lagging | <0.1% of orders |

## Financial Services

### Banking KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Net Interest Margin (NIM) | (Interest Income - Interest Expense) / Assets | Lagging | 2-4% |
| Cost-to-Income Ratio | Operating Costs / Operating Income | Leading | <60% |
| Non-Performing Loan Ratio | NPL / Total Loans | Lagging | <2% |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | Marketing Spend / New Customers | Leading | Varies by segment |
| Cross-Sell Ratio | Products per Customer | Leading | >3 products |

## Department-Level KPIs

### Sales Department
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Sales Pipeline Value | Sum of weighted opportunities | Leading | 3-4x quota |
| Win Rate | Won Deals / Total Opportunities | Lagging | >25% |
| Average Deal Size | Total Revenue / Number of Deals | Lagging | Growth 5-10% YoY |
| Sales Cycle Length | Avg days from lead to close | Leading | Decrease 10% YoY |
| Quota Attainment | Actual Sales / Quota | Lagging | >100% |

### Marketing Department
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Marketing Qualified Leads (MQL) | Leads meeting criteria | Leading | Growth MoM |
| MQL to SQL Conversion | SQL / MQL | Leading | >25% |
| Cost Per Lead (CPL) | Marketing Spend / Leads | Leading | Decrease YoY |
| Marketing ROI | (Revenue - Cost) / Cost | Lagging | >5:1 |
| Brand Awareness | Survey-based metric | Leading | Growth YoY |

### Customer Success Department
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) | Satisfied / Total Responses | Leading | >90% |
| Customer Health Score | Composite metric (usage, engagement) | Leading | >75 average |
| Time to Resolution | Avg time to resolve tickets | Leading | <24 hours |
| Expansion Revenue | Upsell + Cross-sell revenue | Lagging | >20% of ARR |
| Logo Retention Rate | Customers Retained / Starting Customers | Lagging | >90% |

### HR / People Operations
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Employee Net Promoter Score (eNPS) | Promoters - Detractors | Leading | >30 |
| Voluntary Turnover Rate | Voluntary Exits / Headcount | Lagging | <15% |
| Time to Hire | Days from req open to offer accept | Leading | <30 days |
| Offer Acceptance Rate | Offers Accepted / Offers Made | Leading | >85% |
| Training Hours per Employee | Total Training Hours / Headcount | Leading | >40 hours/year |

### Finance Department
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) | (AR / Revenue) × 365 | Leading | <45 days |
| Budget Variance | (Actual - Budget) / Budget | Lagging | <5% |
| Close Cycle Time | Days to close monthly books | Leading | <5 business days |
| Audit Findings | Number of material findings | Lagging | 0 |
| Cash Conversion Cycle | DIO + DSO - DPO | Leading | Minimize |

### IT / Engineering Department
| KPI | Formula | Type | Typical Target |
|-----|---------|------|----------------|
| System Uptime | Uptime / Total Time | Leading | >99.9% |
| Deployment Frequency | Deployments per period | Leading | Daily (for DevOps) |
| Mean Time to Recovery (MTTR) | Avg time to restore service | Leading | <1 hour |
| Change Failure Rate | Failed Changes / Total Changes | Lagging | <15% |
| Sprint Velocity | Story Points Completed / Sprint | Leading | Stable, slight growth |
