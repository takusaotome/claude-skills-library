## Communication Objectives

1. **Awareness**: Ensure all stakeholders understand what is changing and why
2. **Understanding**: Build comprehension of impacts and benefits
3. **Buy-in**: Address concerns and build support
4. **Action**: Guide stakeholders on required actions
5. **Reinforcement**: Sustain new behaviors through ongoing communication

## Key Messages by Phase

### Phase 1: Awareness (Pre-Launch)

| Audience | Key Message | Supporting Points |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| All | What is changing | - Description of change<br>- Timeline<br>- Scope |
| All | Why we are changing | - Business drivers<br>- Market/competitive factors<br>- Risks of not changing |
| All | Who is affected | - Stakeholder groups<br>- Impact levels<br>- Role changes |

### Phase 2: Understanding (Launch)

| Audience | Key Message | Supporting Points |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| All | Benefits of the change | - Organizational benefits<br>- Personal benefits (WIIFM)<br>- Customer benefits |
| Managers | Your role in the change | - Expectations<br>- Support available<br>- Resources |
| End Users | What's in it for me | - How work will improve<br>- New capabilities<br>- Support available |

### Phase 3: Action (Implementation)

| Audience | Key Message | Supporting Points |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| All | What you need to do | - Specific actions<br>- Timeline<br>- Support resources |
| End Users | Training and support | - Training schedule<br>- Help desk<br>- Resources |
| Managers | How to support your team | - Coaching tips<br>- Common issues<br>- Escalation |

### Phase 4: Reinforcement (Post-Launch)

| Audience | Key Message | Supporting Points |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| All | Progress and successes | - Adoption metrics<br>- Success stories<br>- Recognition |
| All | Continuous improvement | - Feedback incorporation<br>- Enhancements<br>- Next steps |

2. Categorize and prioritize
3. Route to appropriate team

5. Incorporate into communications if applicable
6. Report trends to steering committee

[Sender]

Resources: [Link to intranet page]
